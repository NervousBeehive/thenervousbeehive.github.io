Dicejack Dungeon

Dicejack Dungeons is a roguelike dungeon crawler mixed with the card game Blackjack. Players traverse through a randomly generated dungeon trying to reach the exit.

However instead of the traditional method of combat players must duel their enemies in rounds of a modified Blackjack.

Combat Rules:

Both player and enemy must try and reduce their opponents health bar to zero. If the enemy kills the player it is game over

however if the player kills the enemy they will be rewarded with gold, items and crafting materials before moving to the next room

over 12. Each turn the Player will be given the choice to either roll or pass. Once either both opponents have passed or one goes over

12 the winner will deal damage to their opponent based on how much they won by.If either opponent manages to score a perfect 12 they

will perform a critical strike dealing double damage. If both opponents tie nothing happens.

Exploration Rules:

While you traverse the dungeon you will encounter many rooms e.g. monster room, chest room, shop room, trap room, crafting room.
